    About US

    TMS Product operates one of South India's most technologically advanced PET preform manufacturing facilities, strategically located in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. We serve the demanding packaging requirements of beverage, food, and pharmaceutical companies who refuse to compromise on quality. Our facility combines advanced technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce consistent, reliable preforms that ensure optimal blow molding performance for your final packaging needs.

Milacron Technology

At TMS, we use advanced Milacron injection molding technology to ensure precision, efficiency, and consistency in every preform we produce. Known globally for their engineering excellence, Milacron machines help us maintain tight tolerances and high productivity, even under demanding production schedules. By investing in industry leading equipment. we’re able to deliver superior quality preforms that meet the exacting standards of our clients—batch after batch.
