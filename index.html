    About US

    TMS India PVT Ltd is a one-stop solution provider for high-quality PET preforms, serving the diverse packaging needs of the beverage, food, and pharmaceutical industries. Headquartered in India, we operate one of South India's most technologically advanced PET preform manufacturing facilities, strategically located in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh. With cutting-edge manufacturing capabilities and skilled craftsmanship, our facility ensures consistent and reliable preforms that deliver superior blow molding performance, helping our clients maintain the highest packaging standards without compromise.
