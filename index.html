    About US

    With cutting-edge manufacturing and recycling units for plastic granules, TMS India Ltd. has become a world-renowned multinational company. TMS has always been a value-driven organisation, and these cherished values continue to direct our business and growth. We are deeply committed to advancing sustainable practices within the plastic recycling industry, ensuring minimal environmental impact while maximizing the utility of recycled materials. Additionally, TMS is focused on global expansion and continuous innovation, leveraging the latest technologies and strategic partnerships to deliver exceptional products and services that meet the evolving demands of the market.
